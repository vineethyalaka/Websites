MULTIMODAL BIOMETRIC

nVIAsoft provides a highly reliable biometric authentication system based on integrated (all-in –one) authentication. nVIAsoft’s patented technology of multi-modal traits includes hand geometry, hand veins (palm and fingers) palm print pattern recognition technology. Verihand® features industry-leading authentication accuracy with extremely low false rates, and the non-intrusive and totally contactless reader just by seamless hovering the hand over the biometric sensor in a normal distance of 4 to 5 inches away from the sensor. It does not require hand guide that touches the wrist and fingers during enrollment, verification, identification and authentication processes. Our technology provides ease of use, it is highly hygienic with virtually no physiological restriction for all users.

BIOMETRIC REDEFINED

Out of various modalities being tested in the industry with the objective to provide a dynamic authentication using biometric. NVIAsoft’s Verihand stands out among the rest as it is non-invasive and non-intensive, unlike facial recognition that exposes so much privacy and iris (eye reader) biometric that might cause long term vision problem. NVIAsoft’s solution is to provide secured authentication and axiomatic pure digital identity as we say – your secured identity in your very hand [Verihand]. Capturing the whole hand (palm and all four fingerprints) all at once increases the complexity of the data collected enhancing overall security well beyond partial prints, like those captured by sensor-based mobile fingerprint solutions.
